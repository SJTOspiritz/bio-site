Sales & Marketing Lead Ideas

Building an audience and driving leads through social media is key for modern sales strategies. Here are some actionable ideas for Instagram, TikTok, and Telegram outreach:

Instagram Growth

  • Create engaging reels with product demos.
  • Collaborate with micro-influencers in your niche.
  • Use Stories for daily behind-the-scenes updates.
  • Run giveaways and contests to boost engagement.

TikTok Outreach

  • Post short, creative videos showing product use cases.
  • Jump on trending challenges that match your brand style.
  • Utilize TikTok Ads for targeted audiences.
  • Reply to comments with personalized short videos.

Telegram Engagement

  • Create a public group or channel for your brand.
  • Offer exclusive deals and early product drops.
  • Run polls to involve your audience in decisions.
  • Share quick tutorials or tips as voice messages.
